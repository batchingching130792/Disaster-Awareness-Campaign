Pagputok ng Bulkan

When magma is released from a volcano, it allows molten rock, gases and other debris to escape to the surface at speeds up to 100 mph, destroying everything that comes in their path and cause severe health problems.

  • Ipagpatuloy

Tsunami

A series of enormous ocean waves caused by the sudden movement of the ocean surface due to earthquakes, underwater landslides, land slumping into the ocean, volcanic eruptions or meteorite impact in the ocean. It can damage or destroy any type of infrastructure, as well as injure or kill people as waves come in and go out.

  • Ipagpatuloy

Bagyo

Types of tropical cyclones or severe tropical storms. They are giant, rotating storms having an intense spiral system of violent winds that bring rain, thunderstorms, and a union of clouds rotating above tropical or subtropical waters. They can cause extensive damage from heavy rainfall, floods and flying debris from the excessive winds are often the deadly and destructive results.
