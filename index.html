Disasters and Calamities

Earthquakes

The sudden, rapid shaking of the ground caused by the shifting of tectonic plates underneath the Earth's surface or volcanic action, potentially causing great destruction without warning.

Volcanic Eruptions

When magma is released from a volcano, it allows molten rock, gases and other debris to escape to the surface at speeds up to 100 mph, destroying everything that comes in their path and cause severe health problems.
